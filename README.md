COM6601 Group16 Capstone Project: "Bridging or Dividing? Agenda-Setting Effects between Chinese News Media and Social Media on Workplace Mental Health"

1.Data Collection: Crawled and collected all reports related to "workplace mental health" from five major Chinese news media (People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, etc.) and posts Weibo platform in 2025.

2.Data Cleaning: Removed irrelevant content, deduplication, filtered invalid text.

3.Data Analysis: Used R for correlation analysis and lagged regression; employed BERTopic for topic modeling; applied NTUSD/DLUT sentiment lexicons to analyze sentiment valence and specific emotions.
